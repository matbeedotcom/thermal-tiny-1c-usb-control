Enumerator
SHUTTER_PROP_SWITCH 

Auto shutter update switch. 0:OFF, 1:ON.

SHUTTER_PROP_MIN_INTERVAL 

Auto shutter minimun interval,to prevent updates too frequently. unit:1s, range:5-655

SHUTTER_PROP_MAX_INTERVAL 

Auto shutter maximun interval,shutter updates automatically at this time. unit:1s, range:5-655

SHUTTER_PROP_TEMP_THRESHOLD_OOC 

Vtemp's threshold to trigger OOC update in auto shutter.Do OOC update according to the temperature difference between latest update. unit:cnt(36cnt~=1c), range:0-65535

SHUTTER_PROP_TEMP_THRESHOLD_B 

Vtemp's threshold to trigger B update in auto shutter.Do B update according to the temperature difference between latest update. unit:cnt(36cnt~=1c), range:0-65535

SHUTTER_PROP_PROTECT_SWITCH 

Shutter anti-fall protection switch. 0:OFF, 1:ON.

SHUTTER_PROP_ANY_INTERVAL 

Manual shutter to auto shutter(or manual shutter)'s minimun interval, to prevent updates too frequently. unit:1s, range:0-655

SHUTTER_PROTECT_THR_HIGH_GAIN 

Shutter anti-fall protection threshold in high gain mode.Range:0-65535.

SHUTTER_PROTECT_THR_LOW_GAIN 

Shutter anti-fall protection threshold in low gain mode.Range:0-65535.

SHUTTER_PREVIEW_START_1ST_DELAY 

Calling shutter's fisrt delay time after preview start. unit:1s, range:0-655

SHUTTER_PREVIEW_START_2ND_DELAY 

Calling shutter's delay time after fisrt delay. unit:1s, range:0-655

SHUTTER_CHANGE_GAIN_1ST_DELAY

SHUTTER_CHANGE_GAIN_2ND_DELAY 

Calling shutter's fisrt delay time after fisrt delay. unit:1s, range:0-655
