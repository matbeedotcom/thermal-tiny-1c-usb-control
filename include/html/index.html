Libir SDK Introduction

Here are the SDKs of infisense, including:

libirprocess 0.8.2: some image processing algorithm, such as mirror,flip,rotation,and pseudocolor mapping...

libirparse 0.3.1: split the raw frame, and do some data format conversion.

libirtemp 0.13.3_alpha: temperature related calculation, such as: getting the temperature of point, line, and rect, and calculate the parameters of temperature detection.

libiruvc.h 1.10.5_alpha: search the uvc camera, drive it stream.
